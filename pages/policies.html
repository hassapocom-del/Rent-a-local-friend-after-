Policies & Local Policies

Transparent, respectful & professional framework — empowering our local friends & guests

Employment Policies

Application Requirements
  • Graduates of Faculty of Tourism & Hotels or Archaeology
  • Recent criminal record certificate (clean record)
  • Age between 21 and 35 years old
  • Strong ability to interact with tourists; knowledge of extra languages preferred
  • Very good command of English language
  • Good appearance & strong communication skills
  • Ability to work in the field & move easily within Luxor Governorate
Acceptance Criteria
  • Personal interview: evaluates communication, politeness & quick thinking
  • Simple test about Luxor’s attractions & tourist interaction
  • Previous experience = advantage (not mandatory)
Required Documents
  • Copy of National ID card
  • Graduation certificate
  • Recent criminal record certificate
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Recent personal photo

Operational Policies

Work System
  • 8 hours daily – shifts (morning/evening) based on workload
  • One weekly day off
Customer Service
  • Full respect with all tourists
  • Directly asking for tips is strictly prohibited
  • Natural, comfortable experience — like a real local friend
  • Providing false/misleading information is prohibited
Professional Conduct
  • Commitment to attendance & punctuality
  • Clean appearance & appropriate work attire
  • Minimize phone use unless necessary
  • Maintain company image & reputation
Disciplinary Procedures
  • Verbal warning – first violation
  • Written warning – repeated violations
  • Salary deduction or termination for serious misconduct/negligence
Training
  • Pre-work: tourist interaction, Luxor attractions, communication skills
  • Continuous training to improve performance
Evaluation
  • Monthly based on: customer satisfaction, work commitment & performance quality
  • Outstanding employees receive rewards & financial incentives

Tourist & “Local Friend” Relationship Policy

Core Concept

A real local friend + mutual respect + clear boundaries
The goal: a natural experience where the tourist feels they are spending time with a real Egyptian local friend — within a respectful, organized framework.

Interaction Style
  • Friendly, simple, flexible atmosphere
  • Light humor & suggestions for places/experiences are welcomed
  • Plans can be adjusted according to tourist's preferences
  • Main goal: natural, non-artificial experience
Basic Boundaries
🔹 Respect is Mandatory
  • Full commitment to respectful communication
  • Any form of abuse or insult strictly prohibited
  • Respect for local culture, traditions, and religion is essential
🚫 Prohibition of Inappropriate Requests
  • Any unethical, illegal, exploitative, or personally inappropriate request will be immediately rejected & service terminated instantly.
🤝 The Local Friend is Not a Servant
  • Role is companionship & assistance only
  • Not responsible for carrying heavy personal luggage
  • Not obligated to fulfill requests outside the scope of service
🔒 Privacy
  • Pressuring employees about their personal lives is prohibited
  • Photography only allowed with consent of both parties
  • Respecting personal space is essential
In Case of Policy Violations
  • Employees have the right to refuse inappropriate requests & immediately end the tour
  • The company has the right to cancel the service without refund
  • Clients may be banned from using the service in the future
enforced from day one
